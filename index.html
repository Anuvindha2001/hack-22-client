Frequently Asked  Questions

play_arrowWho can participate?

All university students irrespective of any course are eligible to apply.


play_arrowWhat is the registration procedure?

Simply click on "Apply with Devfolio" button on our website and fill out the form. RSVP and other details will be shared closer to the hackathon.


play_arrowCan we apply as a team?

Yes! You can have a maximum of 3 members per team. If you don't have a team, you can participate individually. We'll have team formation at the time of hackathon.


play_arrowWhat does “Application Under Review” mean on Devfolio dashboard?

It means we have successfully received your application. Once you submit your idea after the problem statements are released, we will accept you for our hackathon. Please note that at this stage the submitted ideas will not be evaluated.


play_arrowWhat is the registration fee for hackathon?

Glad you asked. Its completely Free!!!


play_arrowWhat will be the mode of conduct of hackathon?

Due to the current pandemic situation, mode of conduct will be ONLINE this year.


play_arrowWhat do I get by attending .hack?

Pretty much everything from socializing with fellow hacker communities. And yeah we’ll have prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd place. Most of all, you will gain an unparalleled learning experience and memories for a lifetime! You will also get a cool geeky T-Shirt, Certificate of Participation and a bag full of swags.


play_arrowWhat will be the theme for .hack?

Not to worry, the problem statements will be based on a variety of theme options.


play_arrowWhat if I've never been to a hackathon before?

Then we're so glad .hack will be your first ever! It’s helpful to have some programming or technical experience, but it’s not a requirement. We’ll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project.


play_arrowWhere can I find updates?

Join us on Discord and follow us on Instagram for all the updates. Also we will be reaching out to you via mail. To receive the updates through mail, kindly register for the hackathon.
